ABOUT COMMUNION

Holy Communion: Concordia Evangelical Lutheran Church is a member congregation of the Lutheran Church - Missouri Synod. Concordia celebrates Holy Communion in accordance with the Lutheran Teaching in the Real Presence of the Body and Blood of Christ in the Sacrament, and is offered to those who have been baptized, instructed in the Christian faith as confessed by the Lutheran Church with proper preparation on the basis of: Confession of sin; Trust in Christ as Savior; A desire with God's help to amend one's life; And a desire to serve God and neighbor with renewed devotion. Holy Communion is celebrated every Sunday. If you desire to commune but have a question about any of the foregoing, please consult with Pastor before attending the Holy Communion.
