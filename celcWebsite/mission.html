ABOUT OUR MISSION

Our Mission Our desire is to share the message of God's unconditional love and forgiveness with everyone, friends, neighbors and strangers here and around the world. We are Christ-centered and gospel-based in our mission. We are people oriented and not program driven. We are a family of believers and not an institution. We welcome life long Christians as well as seekers who are just beginning a journey of faith. Here at Concordia Lutheran Church you will receive a warm welcome that will tell you that you belong. You are only a stranger once. Come and learn of God's love and explore the Bible with us, in a casual setting. We offer discovery classes that help answer your spiritual questions in a non-threatening, non-judgemental way. "And they preached the good news in that city." Acts 14:21
