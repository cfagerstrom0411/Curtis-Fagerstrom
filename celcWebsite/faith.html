ABOUT OUR FAITH

Our Faith We believe that God the Father created us and all that exists and that He still sustains His creation. He daily provides for all our needs. We believe that Jesus Christ is true God and also true man, has redeemed us lost and condemned creatures, brought us and freed us from sin, death and hell, not with silver or gold, but with His precious blood. We believe that Jesus Christ is the Only way to the Father. Christ died for our sins in order that we might live with Him forever. Through faith we receive forgiveness of sins, and eternal life as free gifts of His grace. We believe that man cannot by his own reason or strength believe in Jesus Christ, or come to Him, however as the Holy Spirit calls us through the Gospel, He creates in us the faith which is necessary to trust in Jesus.
